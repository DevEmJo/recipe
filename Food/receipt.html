Simple Soya Recipe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for an meal. This classic Soya combines sliced meat cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of spices,vegetable or plantains.

Preparation

  • Total: Approximately 10 mins
  • Preparation: 5 mins
  • Cooking: 5 mins

Ingredients

  • 2-3kg of Meat
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional filling cheese, diced vegetable, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Slice the Meat:In a bowl, slice the Cow meat with a pinch of salt and pepper untill they are well sliced.You can add a tablespoon of oil for a oily texture
  2. Heat the pan:Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Fry the Meat:Once the butter is melted and bubbling,pour in the Meat. Tilt the pan to ensure the Meat evenly coat the surface
  4. Add fillings(optional):When the Meat begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over the Meat
  5. Fold and serve:As the Meat continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings.Let it cook for another minute,then slide it onto a plate
  6. Enjoys:Server hot,with additional salt and pepper if needed
